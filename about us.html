ABOUT US

Khaerat's Fashion House deals high-quality tailoring, haute couture fashion, and custom dressmaking for casual and occasional wears. It's a privately owned corporation and deals with different types of wears for women of all age. The business has been in existence for three (3) years and it's located at Lagos state, Nigeria. Khaerat's Fashion House firmly believe that with the right outfit, we can rule the world. Fashion is very important. It is life enhancing and, like everything that gives pleasure, it is worth doing well. Our Core values includes, authenticity, commitment, consistency, friendliness,honesty, loyalty, positivity, perseverance, respect, integrity, and reliability. Style is some each of us already has, all we need to do is find it.
